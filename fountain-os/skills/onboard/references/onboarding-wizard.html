Step 2 of 5
Connect Google Drive

Claude needs access to shared team documents (org context, project files, templates). This requires the Google Drive connector.

How to connect
Open Settings (gear icon, bottom left) then Connectors. Find Google Drive and click Connect. Sign in with your Fountain Google account.

Once connected, type onboard in the chat to restart setup. Claude will verify the connection automatically before continuing.

If you already have Drive connected, just skip ahead

Step 3 of 5
Tell Claude about yourself

Claude is asking you a few questions in the chat right now. Answer them conversationally. Claude will use your answers to build your personal profile.

What Claude will ask
Your name and role, main responsibilities, who you work with, your recurring meetings, and how you like Claude to communicate with you.

Take your time. The more context you give, the better Claude will work for you in future sessions.

This creates your personal CLAUDE.md and memory files

Step 4 of 5
Set up your task board

Type task-setup in the chat. Claude will create your personal task management system.

What gets created
A task board (HTML viewer), a task data file (tasks.json), a build script, and a meeting sync shortcut that automatically captures action items from your Granola meetings.

After setup, your task board opens in this panel. In future sessions, typing triage opens it automatically.
